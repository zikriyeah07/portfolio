Daastan Club Mission

Our mission is to Promote Tourism and culture all over Pakistan and provide feasible ,cost effective ,comfortable and privacy maintained trips to all age and group of people to travel and make their trips as best as possible.

Founded three years ago, Daastan Club has swiftly emerged as the leading travel agency in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (KPK). Our journey began with a simple vision: to offer unparalleled travel experiences that showcase the rich beauty and diverse landscapes of Pakistan. In just a few short years, we have built a reputation for excellence, distinguished by our commitment to exceptional service, personalized itineraries, and a deep-rooted passion for exploration. Our expertise in navigating Pakistan’s stunning terrains—from the majestic peaks of the north to the serene valleys of the south—has solidified our position as the premier choice for travelers seeking both adventure and comfort. At Daastan Club, we are proud to continue our tradition of excellence, dedicated to crafting unforgettable journeys for our clients.
